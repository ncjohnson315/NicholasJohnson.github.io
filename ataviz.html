Founder and President of a data analytics and visualization firm committed to helping all businesses make sense of their data.

Ataviz Consulting was founded on the idea that the understanding and use of data should not be restricted to large corporations. Businesses thrive when they have the ability to discover actionable insights based on their own data. Important business decisions shouldn't be left to gut feelings, they should be informed by data analytics! We provide businesses an affordable option for collecting, analyzing, and/or visualizing their data.

Gone are the days where businesses are left to use poorly formatted spreadsheets that only work half of the time. We are in the midst of the data boom! With so many options at our fingertips, we aren't forced to live in that world anymore. We have the ability to create customized reports and dynamic dashboards that you can access with the click of a button.

Services Offered:

  • Data Collection: Setting up data collection and storage
  • Data Analytics: Analyzing your data and finding trends
  • Data Visualization: Providing custom, automated PDF reports or live, interactive dashboards
  • Business Intelligence: Breaking down the answers to your business questions based solely on data
  • Forecasting: Using statistical analysis to forecast and make predictions
